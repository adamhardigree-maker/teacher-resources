Overview

CVA Teacher Expectations

Use this guide to review the core areas of effective online teaching at CVA. Click a card below to learn more about each expectation area.

 Grading and Feedback Click to learn more 

Grading and Feedback are the expectations that a teacher regularly grades student work and provides timely, meaningful feedback aligned to course standards. Consistent feedback helps students understand their progress and next steps in an asynchronous learning environment.

 Communication and Responsiveness Click to learn more 

Communication and Responsiveness is the expectation that a teacher communicates clearly and consistently so students and families understand expectations and feel supported. The CVA online teacher uses appropriate communication tools and responds within established timeframes to promote engagement and reduce barriers to learning.

 Rapport and Relationships Click to learn more 

Rapport & Relationships is the expectation that a teacher intentionally builds positive connections with students to foster engagement and belonging. The CVA online teacher uses personalized communication and encouragement so students feel known and supported.

 Proactive Intervention and Student Support Click to learn more 

Proactive Intervention and Student Support is the expectation that a teacher monitors student progress and takes timely action when concerns arise. Early outreach and support help prevent students from falling behind and promote academic success.

 Professionalism and Collaboration Click to learn more 

Professionalism and Collaboration reflects that a teacher demonstrates professional conduct and works collaboratively to support student success. Consistent communication and teamwork contribute to a high-quality learning experience for CVA students.
