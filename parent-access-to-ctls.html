CTLS Parent Support

How Parents Can View CTLS Learn

Parents can view their student’s Digital Classroom in CTLS Learn by opening the CTLS Learn link from CTLS Parent. This gives families a helpful view of course information and classroom activity.

Important: Parent access opens in classroom access mode. Parents can view the classroom, but they cannot take assessments, submit assignments, or participate in classroom discussions.

Overview

Parents Can View the Digital Classroom Through CTLS Parent

CTLS Parent gives families a way to access CTLS Learn and see their student’s Digital Classroom. This view helps parents stay informed while keeping student coursework, assessments, submissions, and classroom discussions protected.

Classroom Access Mode

What Parents Can and Cannot Do

✓ Parents Can

  • View the student’s Digital Classroom
  • See classroom information in CTLS Learn
  • Use CTLS Parent to access the CTLS Learn link
  • Stay informed about the student’s online learning environment

✕ Parents Cannot

  • Take assessments
  • Submit assignments
  • Participate in classroom discussions
  • Complete student coursework

Option 1

Using a Website Browser

Step 1

Open CTLS Parent

From Home, select your child in the left sidebar.

Step 2

Find Important Links

Look for the Important Links area on the right side of the page.

Step 3

Click CTLS Learn

Click CTLS Learn to open the student’s Digital Classroom in classroom access mode.

Option 2

Using the CTLS Parent App

Step 1

Open the Menu

From Home, tap the three bars at the top left.

Step 2

Select Students

Select Students in the left column.

Step 3

Choose the Student

Select a student to see their individual information.

Step 4

Tap Dashboard

Tap Dashboard in the middle menu.

Step 5

Open Important Links

Select Important Links.

Step 6

Select CTLS Learn

Tap CTLS Learn to view the student’s Digital Classroom.
