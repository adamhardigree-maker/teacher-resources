Teacher Communication

Contacting Local Schools

CVA teachers should not contact a student’s local school directly. All questions, concerns, requests, or school-related inquiries should be directed to CVA Teacher Support so communication can be handled through the appropriate process.

What to Do

Follow the Right Communication Path

Situation 1

You Have a Question or Concern

Send the question or concern to CVA Teacher Support instead of contacting the student’s local school.

Situation 2

A Local School Contacts You

Forward the email or message to CVA Teacher Support so the request can be handled through the correct CVA channel.

Situation 3

You Are Unsure What to Do

When in doubt, contact CVA Teacher Support first. Do not guess or reach out to the local school directly.

Quick Takeaways

What to Remember

✓

Do not contact a student’s local school directly.

✓

Direct questions, concerns, and inquiries to CVA Teacher Support.

✓

Forward any message from a local school to CVA Teacher Support.

✓

Wait for guidance before taking action or responding.

Bottom Line

Send It to CVA Teacher Support

Whether you have a question, concern, or received a message from a local school, the next step is the same: send it to CVA Teacher Support.
