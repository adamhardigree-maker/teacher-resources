Professionalism & Communication

Teacher Information Page

Every CVA course includes a Teacher Information Page that helps students get to know you and understand how to contact you. Follow the steps below to copy the page into your course, record a brief welcome video in Panopto, and update the page with your information.

Before You Begin

Welcome Video

A short welcome video helps students connect a face and voice with the name they see in the course. Aim for about one to two minutes. Introduce yourself, share something students may find interesting, and remind them that you are available to help.

Accessibility Requirement

All teacher-recorded videos must include accurate captions. Automatically generated captions must be reviewed and corrected before the video is published. Other digital materials must also follow accessible design practices.

Final Review

Final Check

Before publishing the page, open the course in Student View and confirm each item below.

  • The Teacher Information Page opens correctly.
  • The Panopto player loads.
  • The video plays from beginning to end.
  • The audio is clear.
  • The captions appear and are accurate.
  • Students can view the recording without requesting access.
  • Your contact information is correct.
  • Your office hours are correct.
