CTLS Gradebook Support

Quick Scoring in CTLS

Use Quick Scoring when a student’s assessment is stuck as In Progress and the student has verified they cannot reenter the assignment or assessment to click Finish.

Goal: Review the student’s submitted efforts and enter the score the student earned using the Overwrite Score option.

When to Use This

Use Quick Scoring for Locked In Progress Assignments

If a student confirms they cannot reenter an assignment or assessment to click Finish, using Quick Scoring allows the teacher to review the student’s work and enter the earned score directly in the CTLS Gradebook.

Quick Steps

How to Quick Score any student work

Step 1

Open the Gradebook

Go to the CTLS Gradebook and locate the assignment column showing the student’s In Progress status.

Step 2

Click the Grid Icon

Click the grid at the top of the gradebook column to open the Quick Score screen.

Step 3

Review the Work

Review the student’s efforts carefully before entering or adjusting the score.

Step 4

Overwrite the Score

Click Overwrite Score as needed to enter the score the student earned.

Important Reminder

At the end of the term, teachers will be instructed to resolve any In Progress assignments and assessments.
