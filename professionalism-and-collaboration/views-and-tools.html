Views & Tools

The Views & Tools area gives you powerful insights into your students: information that helps you teach more confidently and support them more intentionally.

Big Idea: These tools don't just keep you organized. They help you understand who your students are, what they need, and how you can support them more effectively.

Overview

What Is Views & Tools?

Views & Tools is a dedicated section in CTLS that brings together student data, progress information, and communication management features in one place. It sits in the left-hand navigation of your CTLS Classroom, helping you respond quickly and teach with intention.

Accessing

How to Access Views & Tools

Views & Tools is available directly from your CTLS Classroom or Dashboard. You can access it at any time from the left-hand navigation menu.


As you move through this material, take a few minutes to explore Views & Tools in your own class. This is your chance to build confidence with the systems you use every day and ensure every student gets the attention and care they deserve.


Exploring

What You Will Find

Views & Tools brings together several features that work together to give you a complete picture of your students. Each one surfaces a different layer of information to help you act with intention.

Student Roster

My Students

See a full list of your enrolled students, including their first name and the school they attend. Use the course dropdown to filter by class and the School and Student filters to quickly locate specific students. The Export button lets you download your roster for planning and communication purposes.

Monitoring Progress

Progress Tracker

Monitor where each student is in the course relative to the Class Schedule. The Progress Tracker helps you identify students who are falling behind, missing work, or disengaged — so you can reach out before a concern becomes a crisis.

Data & Reporting

Additional Views

Views & Tools contains additional reporting and analytics views accessible within the section. Explore your own class in CTLS to discover the full set of tools and build confidence with the information available to you each week.

Reference

The My Students View

When you open Views & Tools and select My Students, you'll see a roster of your enrolled students. Here's how to read the screen and use its controls.

1

Course Dropdown

Filter the student list by course. Select any class you teach to see only those enrolled students.

2

Select School Filter

Filter by the student's home school. Useful when your class includes students from multiple Cobb County schools.

3

Student Filter

Search or filter by individual student name to quickly locate a specific student in your roster.

4

Export Button

Download your student roster data. The export includes First Name and School columns and can be used for communication planning and reporting.

5

Student List

The main table shows each student's first name and home school. Students from multiple Cobb County schools may appear in a single class.

Why It Matters

Teaching with Intention

In an asynchronous online course, the data in Views & Tools is often the earliest signal you have that a student needs support. A student who stops showing up in the roster data, falls behind on progress, or accumulates missing work needs outreach — not a waiting period. Regular use of Views & Tools is what makes proactive, student-centered teaching possible at CVA.
