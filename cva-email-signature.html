Unit Three: Communication & Responsiveness

Required Email Signature

Every CCSD employee and CVA Adjunct Teacher is required to use a standard email signature format. This helps keep communication professional, consistent, and easy for students and families to recognize.

Directions: Download the MS Word file for the CCSD Email Signature and update the template with your own information.

Overview

Use the Standard CCSD Email Signature Format

Your email signature should follow the approved format so that all CVA communication looks consistent and includes the correct contact information. Use the template file below to create or update your signature.

What to Update

Customize the Template With Your Information

Field 1

Name and Title

Replace the sample name and job title with your own professional information.

Field 2

CVA Information

Keep the Cobb Virtual Academy name, address, work phone, and website listed in the template.

Field 3

Contact Details

Update the cell phone and email address fields as needed.

Field 4

Logo and Formatting

Do not remove the CCSD logo or change the overall signature format.
