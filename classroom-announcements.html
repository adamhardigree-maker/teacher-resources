Digital Classroom Setup

Class Board

When students first enter your Digital Classroom in CTLS, the Class Board is already visible. Board Topics and Announcements help you set the tone, share important information, and create teacher presence from the start.

Big Idea: The Class Board gives you a visible space to engage, inform, guide, and support students throughout the course.

Overview

Two Types of Class Board Entries

Students will see two types of entries on the Class Board: Board Topics and Announcements. Both help students know where to find information, what to pay attention to, and how to stay connected to the course.

Class Board Tools

Board Topics vs. Announcements

Ongoing Information

Board Topics

Board Topics are designed for information students may need to revisit throughout the term. They are a good place to organize helpful resources, course reminders, support information, and other content students may need more than once.

Time-Sensitive Updates

Announcements

Announcements are time-sensitive posts meant to get students’ attention right away. They appear on the student Dashboard and send a notification to the CTLS Parent app, making them hard to miss.

Board Topic Highlight

CVA News You Can Use

CVA will automatically post a Board Topic titled CVA News You Can Use in your Digital Classroom. This topic is updated regularly with program-wide information students may need during the term.

DIRECTIONS: Use the arrows to click through the CVA News You Can Use posts.

Announcement Ideas

Use Announcements to Build Presence and Support Students

Show Your Personality

Use a warm tone, quick video, or personal note to help students see the human behind the course.

Connect to Current Events

Share timely articles, videos, or examples that connect directly to your course content.

Support Learning

Post district-vetted videos, readings, or practice opportunities to revisit challenging concepts.

Clarify Directions

Break down multi-step tasks, highlight common questions, or model expectations.

Recognize Success

Celebrate student achievements or spotlight professional models to motivate learners.

Share Success Tips

Reinforce due dates, pacing suggestions, and study strategies to help students stay on track.

Helpful Features

Schedule and Target Announcements

Announcements can be scheduled with a start and end date, and they automatically post to the Digital Classroom calendar. You can also limit visibility to specific students for targeted reminders, make-up work, or individual follow-ups.

Teacher Move: Use announcements strategically so students receive the right message at the right time.
