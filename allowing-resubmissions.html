CTLS Gradebook Support

Resubmission Opportunities

Use this process when a student is eligible to revise and resubmit graded work. The original attempt should be graded first before a new attempt is opened.

Goal: Finish the original attempt, allow a resubmission, mark the new attempt as missing, and clearly communicate the resubmission deadline in the feedback box.

Overview

Open a New Attempt After the Original Attempt Has Been Graded

Before allowing a resubmission, grade the student’s original attempt and finish the student’s submission. Then reopen the graded work and allow the student to resubmit according to the resubmission expectations.

Quick Steps

How to Allow a Resubmission

Step 1

Grade the Original Attempt

Review and grade the student’s original submission first.

Step 2

Click Finish Student

After grading the original attempt, click Finish Student.

Step 3

Reopen the Graded Work

Open the graded submission again so the resubmission option is available.

Step 4

Allow Resubmission

Click Allow Resubmission to open a new attempt for the student.

Step 5

Mark the New Attempt Missing

Mark the new resubmission attempt as Missing until the student submits the revised work.

Step 6

Add the Deadline

Enter a Resubmission Deadline in the feedback box so the expectation is clear.

Step 7

Click Finish Student Again

Click Finish Student to save the resubmission setup.

Important Reminder

Students may not have any missing coursework to be eligible for resubmission attempts.

Before opening a resubmission opportunity, confirm that the student does not have missing coursework. Resubmission attempts should only be allowed when the student meets the eligibility expectations.
